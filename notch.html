Notch

San Diego, CA | March 2019 - July 2019

In collaboration with: Christina Ding


Notch is a small non-profit organization focused on helping nursing mothers by cleaning and redistributing used breast pumps. In March 2019, I began working with Notch as their web designer. As the web designer, worked alongside the organization's creator to design their website, logo, and icons using Wix and Photoshop. Throughout the entire process, I applied my own design principles while fulfilling the requested features for the website. Notch was launched in July, and I continue to oversee website and social media needs for the organization. Check out the site (and please consider donating!)
