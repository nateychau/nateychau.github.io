Christians at UCSD

San Diego, CA | February 2019 - April 2019


From February to April 2019, I worked with two fellow club members to update the Christian at UCSD’s club website on WordPress. The website was handed off to us from previous club members (now alumni), and we were tasked with modernizing and improving it. Our changes included adding new “Who We Are” and “What We Do” pages, updating the layout and design of existing pages, and replacing all of the out-dated photos throughout the website with new ones.
